Book Review: 12 Rules Of Life

Author: Jordan Peterson

Rating: ★★★★★

Genre:

Themes: growth, self-help

Format:

Finished: May 11, 2019

Purchase link

The ideas in the book are quite interesting. He lays out his arguments on focusing yourself, inward, rather than only worrying about the world, outward.

Having the said, the writing could have been much better and book quite a bit shorter.
