Gifts

Sep 12, 2007


Give a man
sorrow, the darkness.
But don’t take
dreams he possess.

Give a man
pain, the heartbreak.
But don’t take
his desire to stay awake.

Give a man
greed, the lust.
But don’t take
his love just.

Give a man
tomorrow, today.
But don’t take
his fading yesterday.
