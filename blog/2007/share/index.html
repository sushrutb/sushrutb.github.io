Share

Aug 20, 2007

Shared my laughter,
my joy, my smile
But how do I share
the twinkle in my eyes?

Shared my dreams,
my goals, my aim.
But how do I share
the passion in my heart?

Shared my affection,
my love, my longings.
But how do I share
skipping beat of the heart?

Shared my music,
my rhythm, my words.
But how do I share
beats of my heart?

Shared my sorrows,
my loss, my grief.
But how do I share
tears in my eyes?

Shared my beliefs,
my god, my religion.
But how do I share
the untouched soul?
