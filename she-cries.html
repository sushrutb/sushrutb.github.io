She cries

Nov 16, 2021

She cries. Because she likes reading, when it rains. Even on a beautiful summer day.

She cries. Because tears are more familiar. Even in a warm embrace of her lover.

She cries. Because happiness feels fleeting. But sadness will last her a lifetime.

She cries.
